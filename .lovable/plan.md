

# Maison Haimi - Luxury Dental Practice Website

## Overview
A serene, editorial-style website for Dr. Eliza Haimi's boutique dental practice, featuring elegant typography, a cream and champagne gold color palette, and luxury fashion-inspired layouts.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Home Page

### Design System Setup
- Implement the "Maison Haimi" color palette (Cream, Soft Sand, Charcoal Silk, Brushed Champagne Gold)
- Configure Cormorant Garamond for headlines and Montserrat Light (300) for body text
- Create reusable luxury-style components (buttons, section layouts, transitions)

### Navigation
- Sticky header that transitions from transparent to opaque on scroll
- Elegant navigation with links: Home, The Practice, The Atelier (Services), Concierge
- Mobile: Refined hamburger menu with the practice phone as a fixed footer button

### Hero Section (Home)
- Split-screen layout: Large lifestyle image on left, clean text on right
- Headline: "The Art of the Conservative Smile"
- Sub-headline introducing Dr. Eliza Haimi and the Great Neck location
- Elegant "Begin Your Journey" CTA button

---

## Phase 2: The Practice (About Page)

### Philosophy Section
- "A Relationship-First Approach" headline
- Editorial layout showcasing conservative treatment philosophy
- Highlight 5-star reputation and gentle dentistry approach for dental phobia

### The Studio Section
- Architectural showcase of the 7 Bond St. location
- Elegant imagery and descriptive text about the practice environment

---

## Phase 3: The Atelier (Services Page)

### Three Service Galleries
- **The Aesthetic Gallery**: Smile makeovers, porcelain veneers, whitening, dental bonding
- **The Restoration Studio**: Implants, crowns, bridges, root canal therapy
- **The Foundation**: Checkups, cleanings, oral cancer screenings, sealants
- **Specialty Care**: Invisalign and Emergency Services

### Editorial Layouts
- Large imagery paired with service descriptions (no bullet points)
- Soft transitions between service categories
- Each service presented with artful, magazine-style formatting

---

## Phase 4: The Patient Concierge (Resources/Contact)

### New Patient Section
- Clean, minimalist "Access Digital Intake" button
- Sophisticated text about insurance partnerships and financing

### Location & Hours
- Grayscale embedded map of Great Neck
- Operating hours displayed elegantly (Mon/Tue 8-5, Wed 8-1, Thu 8-4:30)

### Multi-Step Appointment Form
- Beautiful one-question-at-a-time booking experience
- Smooth transitions between steps
- Fields: Name, contact info, service interest, preferred time, special requests
- Email notification sent to your practice upon submission
